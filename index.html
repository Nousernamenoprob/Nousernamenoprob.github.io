About Me

My name is Maddie Watts. I am 21 years old, born and raised in southeast Michigan to a single mother. I was on the debate team in high school and did 13 years of dance, non-competitive more as a hobby. I am getting an associates in software engineering though I am leaning back into getting a history degree as that is a passion of mine. I work at a latchkey during the school year and a summer camp during the summer, I also babysit and petsit on the side. In my free time I love bike riding (my favorite route is close to 10 miles long) reading, listening to music, watching movies, photography, and playing the Sims. I have a yearly goal to read 25 books, listen to 150 new albums, and watch 100 new movies. Along with my commputer classes I have taken other CIS classes for fun, such as Intro to Python and Web Design
